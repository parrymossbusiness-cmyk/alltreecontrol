Stump & Debris Services in Little Rock, AR

Stump grinding, storm cleanup, and debris hauling that gets your yard back to normal fast — with the same crew that handled the removal, or on its own.

A finished tree removal or a passing storm often leaves behind more than just a stump — root flare, wood chips, broken limbs, and piles of brush that need to go somewhere. All Tree Control handles the entire back half of tree work: grinding stumps flush with the ground, clearing storm debris, hauling brush, and leaving the property looking like the job never happened, aside from the improvement.

Stump and debris work moves fast once scheduled. Most stump grinding jobs are finished within a couple of hours, and storm cleanup crews are dispatched quickly because we know a downed limb blocking a driveway or a pile of debris in the yard isn't something you want to look at for long. Pricing depends mainly on stump diameter, root spread, ground access, and total debris volume — all things we can assess accurately with a quick on-site visit, whether or not we did the original tree work.

Stump Removal

Stump removal goes further than grinding — instead of chewing the stump down below grade, we pull the entire stump and major root ball out of the ground. This is the right call when you're planning to plant a new tree in the exact same spot, install a pool or foundation, or simply don't want any wood decomposing underground where it can attract termites or fungus over time. Full removal leaves a larger hole than grinding, which we backfill with soil so the area is ready for landscaping right away. It's a more involved job than grinding, especially for large hardwood stumps with deep taproots common in older Little Rock neighborhoods, so we scope it on-site to give you an accurate price and timeline.

Brush Removal

Overgrown brush piles, fence-line thickets, and years of accumulated yard waste take real equipment to clear efficiently. Our brush removal service handles everything from a single pile left over from a weekend of yard work to acres of overgrown fence line that haven't been touched in years. We chip what can be chipped on-site and haul away what can't, and we're equipped to get into tight side yards and back corners that a rented chipper or a weekend of manual labor can't reach efficiently. Brush removal is also a common add-on when we're already on-site for a tree removal or land-clearing job, since it's far more efficient to clear everything in one visit.

Debris Hauling & Cleanup

Not every debris job starts with us — sometimes a homeowner has already cut up a fallen tree or trimmed back overgrowth and just needs it gone. Our debris hauling and cleanup service picks up wood, branches, chips, and general yard debris and hauls it off your property entirely, whether it's from our own work or a project you handled yourself. We can also do a final cleanup pass after any tree job, blowing off driveways and walkways and raking up smaller debris that a chipper leaves behind, so the property looks finished rather than just "worked on."

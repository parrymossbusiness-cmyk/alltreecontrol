Storm Cleanup in Little Rock, AR

Rapid removal of downed limbs, fallen trees, and storm debris from your yard, driveway, and roofline.

Storm cleanup is what comes after the emergency call — once a dangerous tree has been dealt with, or if the storm left behind broken limbs and debris without an active hazard, you're left with a yard full of wood, leaves, and mess that needs to go. We treat storm cleanup calls as priority work, since we know most homeowners want their property back to normal as fast as possible, and prolonged debris piles can attract pests or become a hazard themselves.

Central Arkansas storms tend to hit hardest in spring and early summer, when fast-moving thunderstorm lines and occasional tornado warnings bring down limbs across entire neighborhoods at once. During these high-demand periods, we prioritize calls based on severity — driveway-blocking debris and anything resting on a structure move to the front of the line, while general yard debris gets scheduled as quickly as our crews can manage. We coordinate closely with homeowners on realistic timelines during major storm events rather than making promises we can't keep.

Cleanup includes cutting down oversized debris into manageable sections, chipping what can be chipped, hauling away what can't, and a final pass to clear smaller branches and leaf litter from lawns, driveways, and gutters where accessible. If a fallen tree also needs a stump ground down, we can handle that in the same visit or schedule it separately.

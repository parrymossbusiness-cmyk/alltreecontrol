Tree Service in Benton, AR

Licensed, insured removal, trimming, and stump grinding for homeowners across Saline County.

Benton, the seat of Saline County, sits along I-30 southwest of Little Rock and combines older neighborhoods near downtown and the Saline River with newer subdivisions pushing outward toward Alcoa Road and beyond. That mix means our crews handle a real range of work here, from mature oaks and pines that have stood for decades near the historic downtown square to younger, still-developing trees in newer developments where good early structural pruning pays off for years to come.

Saline County's terrain includes a good amount of wooded land, and Benton's continued residential growth has kept our land-clearing and lot-preparation crews busy as new construction moves into previously forested areas. The Saline River and its tributaries also mean some properties deal with wetter soil conditions than you'd find further into Little Rock proper, which we factor into root health assessments and can affect how a tree responds to drought or disease over time.

We serve all of Benton and the surrounding communities of Alexander, Bryant, and Haskell, giving us a strong presence throughout Saline County alongside our core Little Rock metro coverage.

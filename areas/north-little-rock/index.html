Tree Service in North Little Rock, AR

Licensed, insured removal, trimming, and stump grinding for homeowners across the river in Pulaski County.

North Little Rock sits just across the Arkansas River from the capital, and its established neighborhoods — Park Hill, Lakewood, Rose City, and the areas around Burns Park — carry some of the most mature tree canopy in the metro. Many homes here date back several decades, and the oaks and pines planted alongside them have grown large enough that routine maintenance now often means real structural work: crown reduction, deadwood removal, and occasional emergency response when a limb finally gives out.

Because North Little Rock's older neighborhoods were built before modern setback standards, it's common to find large trees growing just a few feet from a foundation or driveway, which changes how we approach both trimming and removal. Our crews plan rigging and drop zones carefully in these tighter lots, and we're familiar with the mix of housing stock across the city, from the historic homes near Park Hill to newer construction further north toward the Camp Robinson area.

We serve all of North Little Rock and stay close to our neighbors in Sherwood, Jacksonville, and Little Rock, which lets us respond quickly to storm damage on either side of the river. Whether it's a single overgrown tree or a full-property assessment after severe weather, our team knows the neighborhoods and the trees in them.
